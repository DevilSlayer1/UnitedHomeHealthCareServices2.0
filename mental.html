Welcome to the Mental Health Checker

This is a simple Mental Health Assessment app which will more or less diagonise the presence of mental health problem. Nevertheless, you are always encouraged to visit a mental healthcare provider or a therapist in case your mental health worsens or feel suicidal.

Click the start quiz button below to start the mental health checker. Beware, you cannot skip a question or revisit an already answered question. The result is shown after all the questions have been answered.
